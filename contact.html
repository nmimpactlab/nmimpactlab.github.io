Corrections

Everything we publish is drawn from public records, and records can contain errors — theirs or ours. If you believe something here is inaccurate, email research@nmimpactlab.org with the page and the specific detail, and we will review it and correct the record where warranted.

Tips & records

If you have public records or a tip that belongs in the public interest, we want to hear from you. Please share only information you are lawfully able to share, and tell us if you need your identity kept confidential.

Please do not send confidential, privileged, or sealed material through ordinary email. If a matter is sensitive, email us first and we can discuss a safer channel.
