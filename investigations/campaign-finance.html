Campaign Finance

Money in politics · In progress

New Mexico's campaign-finance disclosures show who funds candidates and committees — but the filings are spread across cycles and formats that make cross-year analysis hard. This project normalizes contributions and expenditures into consistent, searchable records.

What it covers

  • Contributions to candidates and political committees.
  • Expenditures reported by those committees.
  • Consistent donor and committee names to enable search across cycles.

Sources

Records originate from New Mexico's official campaign-finance disclosure system. Cleaned values are published alongside references to the original filings.

Status: Data collection and normalization are underway. Personal information beyond what disclosure law requires is withheld. Questions or a correction? research@nmimpactlab.org.

What's next

A searchable interface to look up a candidate, committee, or donor and see the money flowing in and out, with links to source filings.
