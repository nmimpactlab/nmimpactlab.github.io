Campaign Finance

Money in politics · Live

New Mexico's campaign-finance disclosures show who funds candidates and committees — but the filings are spread across cycles and formats that make cross-year analysis hard. This project normalizes contributions and expenditures into consistent, searchable records.

Explore $248M in NM campaign money (2020–2026) →

What it covers

  • Contributions to candidates and political committees.
  • Expenditures reported by those committees.
  • Consistent donor and committee names to enable search across cycles.

Sources

Records originate from New Mexico's official campaign-finance disclosure system. Cleaned values are published alongside references to the original filings.

Now live: an interactive tool covering $248M raised and $213M spent, 2020–2026 — leaderboards for top organizational donors and sectors, and profiles for 2,700+ candidates and committees. To protect privacy, individual donors are pseudonymized and their personal identifiers removed; organizations and recipients (public actors) are named. Corrections: research@nmimpactlab.org.

What's next

Adding donor-flow networks, per-cycle comparisons, and links from each recipient to its source filings.
