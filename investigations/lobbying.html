Lobbying & Employers

Influence · Live

Registered lobbyists shape legislation on behalf of paying clients. This project cross-indexes lobbyists with the employers who hire them, making it possible to see who is working which issues — and for whom.

Search 4,400+ lobbyist–employer registrations →

What it covers

  • Registered lobbyists and the employers/clients they represent.
  • The link between an employer and every lobbyist working for it.
  • Searchable indexes in both directions: by lobbyist and by employer.

Sources

Records originate from New Mexico's official lobbyist registration and disclosure filings. Each entry references its source filing.

Now live: a searchable cross-index of 4,400+ lobbyist–employer registrations. Search by either side — a lobbyist to see their employers, or an employer to see everyone lobbying on its behalf. As a privacy matter, we publish only the public registration facts (name, employer, employer city/state, years) and omit personal contact details such as email, phone, and home address. Corrections: research@nmimpactlab.org.

What's next

Extending coverage to additional registration years and connecting employers to the bills and committees they lobbied.
