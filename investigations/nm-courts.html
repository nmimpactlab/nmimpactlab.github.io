Court & Financial Records

Courts · Planned

New Mexico's court systems generate public records that are valuable for understanding how the justice system operates — but they are difficult to analyze in aggregate. This project structures court and related financial records for transparency and analysis.

What it will cover

  • Publicly available court records and dockets.
  • Associated financial data, where public.
  • Consistent structure to support aggregate analysis.

Sources

Records will originate from New Mexico's public court systems. As with every project, sources will be preserved and linked.

Status: This investigation is in planning. We are scoping which records are public, how to collect them responsibly, and how to present them. Interested or have a lead? research@nmimpactlab.org.
